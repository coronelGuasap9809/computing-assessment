What is cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the state of being safe on digital networks such as the internet. It is important to stay cyber-secure in order to protect your sensitive data and other important virtual assets (such as your bank account!). Ways to increase your cybersecurity online include:

  • setting strong passwords
  • protecting yourself from unwanted connections
  • guarding against phishing
  • creating backups
  • making an effort to stay safe on social media

Why use this site?

This site was specifically made to inform you, the users, on how to increase your cyberscurity using data, statistics and visuals such as graphics and images. This site addresses some of the most common issues and aspects of cybersecurity that the average user of the internet encounters on a near-daily basis. Here, you can find a total of 5 of these aspects, which where mentioned in the What is Cybersecurity? section above. To view more info and select one of these, go to the Aspects page.
